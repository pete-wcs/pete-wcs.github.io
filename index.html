Shop Opening Soon

Winford Community Shop is nearly ready to open! Volunteers, the 100+ members and the steering group have been working hard since last Autumn to fill the staffing rota, order stock and complete all the legalities. We now hope to open by the end of May and will publicise a launch date soon.

The community-run shop will have a sustainable ethos, avoiding plastic bags and offering a good range of fruit and vegetables, groceries, take away pies, frozen ready meals, cleaning products available for refills, pet food, wine, cigarettes and much more.

Any comments can be sent to winfordcommunityshop@gmail.com

Shares in the Shop

Thank you to everyone who has pledged to support the community shop and post office. So far we have pledges for just under £27,000 from 100+ people. Our target is to raise £30,000 and we are delighted that so many people want to be a part of this new venture.

If you missed the chance to pledge, you can still buy shares. Shareholders will become members of the Shop society and have a say in how the shop is run. You only need to buy one share at £25.00 each to have full voting rights.

The Steering Group have put together a Business Plan setting out the plans and financial forecasts for the shop. You can view a copy here. You may have heard that Winford Village Store and Post Office is set to close, since it is no longer commercially viable in its present form. Whilst we don’t know when exactly, it is likely to be very soon.

If a solution is to be found it will need everyone to pull together. If you value the services a village shop and/or post office can offer the community and want something to replace it, then you must get involved.
