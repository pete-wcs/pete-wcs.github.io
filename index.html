You may have heard that Winford Village Store and Post Office is set to close, since it is no longer commercially viable in its present form. Whilst we don’t know when exactly, it is likely to be very soon.

If a solution is to be found it will need everyone to pull together. If you value the services a village shop and/or post office can offer the community and want something to replace it, then you must get involved.

Share Offer now open!

Thank you to everyone who has pledged to support the community shop and post office. So far we have pledges for just under £27,000 from 70 people. Our target is to raise £30,000 and we are delighted that so many people want to be a part of this new venture. We would like to increase the number of shareholders to 100 and raise a further £3000.

The share offer is now open and will run until Monday 11th February 2019. If you missed the chance to pledge, you can still buy shares. Shareholders will become members of the Shop society and have a say in how the shop is run. You only need to buy one share at £25.00 each to have full voting rights.

Our plans for the transfer of ownership are progressing well and we hope to have everything in place to take over the running of the shop in March.
