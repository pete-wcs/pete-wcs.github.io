Have your say

Winford village shop is closing! The plan is to replace it with a community-run shop which will be managed and staffed by volunteers. This is an ideal opportunity to create something that will serve the community in the best way possible, but in order to make it a reality we need input from the community - this is where you come in! Please use the form below to let us know what you would like to see from this project and to put forward any ideas and suggestions that you may have.
